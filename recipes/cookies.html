Brownie Mix Cookies

Description

Made with brownie mix, these soft, chewy cookies are delicious â€” and they're quick and simple too!

Ingredients

  • 1 (19.8 ounce) package brownie mix
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Lightly grease a baking sheet.
  2. Mix brownie mix, flour, oil, eggs, and water together in a large bowl; stir in chocolate chips. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto the prepared baking sheet.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until cookies are just set, about 9 minutes. Let cool on baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
